企业理念

科技为根本，人才为核心，创新为驱动，振兴为使命
Science and technology as the root, professional as the core, innovation as the drive, revitalization as the mission。

企业愿景

在满足质量管理体系要求下完成PMA，STC和MDA CCAR-145的取证,为公司快速健康发展打下坚实基础；
Completion of PMA,STC and MDA and CCAR-145 certification under the quality management system requirements has laid a solid foundation for the company's rapid and healthy development;
营收达到关键指标使公司进入良性持续发展期。
Revenue achieve the key indicators, the company enters a favorable and sustainable development period。

沿革

2014年
2014 年 7 月 17 日成立于辽宁省大连市。
 
The company was founded in 2014 in Dalian, Liaoning Province, CHN。
2023年
2023 年在东北振兴以及国家航空产业布局背景下公司增加申请PMA、MDA、STC和CCAR-145。
 
In 2023, in the context of Northeast China‘s revitalization and the layout of the national aviation industry, the company’s business promote to applications for CCAR-21 PMA, MDA, STC and CCAR-145。
2024年
招揽业内资深航空人士壮大公司实力与竞争力。
 
Recruiting aviation industry professionals to expand the company‘s competence and competitiveness。
